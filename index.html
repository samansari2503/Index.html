If you find yourself in a conversation about dieting or weight loss, chances are you will hear of the ketogenic, or keto, diet. That is because the keto diet has become one of the most popular methods worldwide to shed excess weight and improve health. What is basically a Keto Diet ??

Images:

Wanna lose weight while eating your favourite food. Join us, we will provide you one of the best diet plans in the world. You can watch various people reviews towards keto Diet plan. The keto diet is essentially a high-fat diet your meals are 70 or 80% fat; about 20% protein; and about 5% carbohydrate. It is not an Atkins high-protein diet. The keto diet switches you from burning glucose (which carbs provide) to burning ketones (which fat produces) for energy.
