If you find yourself in a conversation about dieting or weight loss, chances are you will hear of the ketogenic, or keto, diet. That is because the keto diet has become one of the most popular methods worldwide to shed excess weight and improve health. Research has demonstrated that adopting this low-carb, high-fat diet can promote fat loss and even improve certain conditions such as type 2 diabetes and cognitive decline.
